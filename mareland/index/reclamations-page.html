About MaReLand Reclamations

“Reclamations” is a blog facilitated by the MaReLand project.
The website sets to be a free networking platform bringing together researchers, activists, and sea aficionados interested in land reclamation processes. It is a place where everybody can share field stories, ideas, findings, perspectives, and ongoing activities on related topics in Malaysia, the broader Asian region, and beyond. The Reclamations blog also provide a space where to quickly publish beyond academic venues.

Welcome to Reclamations: The MaReLand Blog!

For further information and details on how to submit a post, please contact the editor at pierpa.degios@zoho.com or pierpaolo.degiosa@upol.cz.
